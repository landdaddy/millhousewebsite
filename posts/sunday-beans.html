Sunday Beans with Lemon Oil

There are days when a house needs a low simmer more than a candle. These beans are simple, generous, and happy under a torn piece of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for finishing
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced

Method

  1. Warm olive oil in a heavy pan. Add onion and cook until soft.
  2. Add garlic, rosemary, chili flakes, beans, and a splash of water. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Finish with lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, and more olive oil.
